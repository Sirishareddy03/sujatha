ABOUT THE EVENT

Mr Miss Mrs India Gems is a concept to apperceive the vigour & elegance of all the individuals who come under this title. We are visionaries with team Mrs Nirali Pandya & Mrs Dharti Joshi. Mr Miss Mrs India Gem is not merely competitions but an effort to bring the men & women of India to fore and present their talent to the world in an elegant manner. It gives every deserving participants a chance to become a model or a celebrity. Eminent personalities from around the country come to judge their talent and skills. We aim to empower women & men and give them the platform to showcase their beauty, intelligence and wisdom.
